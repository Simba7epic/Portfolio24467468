Endpoint Detection and Response (EDR):
	-Detect connected endpoints and assess their risks
	-Continuous monitoring of laptops, desktops, servers, and IoT devices

CrowdStrike Falcon:
•	Real-time threat detection and prevention 
•	AI-driven behavioural analysis 
•	Advanced threat hunting capabilities 
•	Cloud-native scalability and rapid deployment


Microsoft Defender for Endpoint:
•	Automated attack surface reduction 
•	Endpoint behavioural monitoring 
•	AI-assisted threat investigation and prioritisation 
•	Seamless integration with Microsoft 365 and Azure environments 


SentinelOne Singularity:
•	Autonomous detection and response 
•	AI-based behavioural threat detection 
•	Endpoint rollback and recovery capabilities 
•	Offline protection and containment 



Comparison:
•	CrowdStrike Falcon - speed, scalability, and cloud-native threat intelligence, but is expensive
•	Microsoft Defender - Endpoint focuses on ecosystem integration and enterprise security management, but has Microsoft ecosystem dependency
•	SentinelOne Singularity - autonomous detection and automated remediation, but has less flexible tuning
